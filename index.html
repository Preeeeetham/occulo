What we do

We give space a sense of itself.

01

Space-based analysis,
not headcount

Existing systems count people. We measure how a space is occupied — its density, its distribution, its available capacity at any given moment. Counting arrivals tells you who entered. Spatial analysis tells you whether the space can safely absorb more.

02

Signals that arrive
before the incident

Overcrowding is not an event. It is a condition that builds. OCCULO reads that condition continuously — so the people responsible for a space can act on it while there is still time to act.

03

Retrofit into
existing environments

OCCULO is designed to be added to an environment, not to replace it. It integrates with what is already operational. No structural changes. No extended deployment windows.

04

No personal data,
by design

Spatial analysis does not require identity. OCCULO processes environmental conditions — density, distribution, flow — not individuals. No faces. No tracking. No data that could be personal.
